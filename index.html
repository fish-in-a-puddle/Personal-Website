This is a website full of my thoughts and ideas, my reviews of stuff I like, a gallery of my projects and honestly who knows what else. Don't worry about it. It'll be fun!

About me

I'm a Canadian high schooler working on a lot of different things at the same time. I like robots and space and I'm going to be running my school robotics club in September. Apart from robotics, some of my other projects include a quilt made from some of my favorite old t-shirts and embroidered quilt squares and a Project Hail Mary diorama in a wooden book. I have very little experience with HTML, CSS and Javascript, but I thought it would be fun to learn some.

My projects
